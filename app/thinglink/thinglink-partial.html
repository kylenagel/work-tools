Thinglink

Link: Thinglink
What it does: Thinglink allows interactive points to be loaded onto any image. When those images are embedded on webpages, the user will be able to hover over a point on the image and read the infomation entered.

How to make one

1. Upload your image

  • In the main menu bar, click the "CREATE" button (red with a plus sign).
  • You can upload from your hard drive, from Facebook, from Flickr or from the web.
  • For a hard drive image, click or drag-and-drop onto the blue bar that reads "Choose images or drag-and-drop here." Choose the correct image.
  • Once your image is loaded, Thinglink will automatically take you to the editing area.

2. Add tags (interactive points) to your image

  • Click on your image where you want the interactive point to be.
  • In the panel on the left, add a link or url to an image, if wanted.
  • In the panel on the left, click the icon to bring up a menu of available icons. Choose which one you would like.
  • Write the text you want to make available with that icon into the text field. Some minor styling options are available.
  • Click "Save Tag."

3. Save get embed code

  • When finished placing tags, click the green "Save" button in the bottom right.
  • In the menu on the right, click the "Share" button.
  • Cooy the code in the "Embed" window, which will be used to place the newly interactive image on our sites.
