Canva

Link: Canva
What it does: Canva provides you with templates and tools to create static infographics and designs.

How to make one

1. If needed, set up a free account

  • Canva will allow you to save and share your projects.

2. Choose the type of dimensions you want to work with

  • Click on the "Your Designs" tab in the top menu.
  • Choose from the popular dimension types or click the "More..." button on the right to see more.
  • Click on the type of design you want to set the dimensions.
  • If you can't find the dimensions you want, click "Use custom dimentions" from the main "Your Designs" tab and define your dimensions.

3. Build your design

  • Once you have your empty canvas, you can add elements from the menu in the left rail (some are free, and some have a cost listed):
    • SEARCH: Allows you to search from various elements to add to your cannvas.
    • LAYOUTS: Gives you a starting point to add your custom content.
    • TEXT Allows you to add text from existing templates or style it on your own.
    • BKGROUND: Allows you to add a background color, photo or design to your canvas.
    • UPLOADS: Upload an image from your computer or from Facebook.
  • Use the Canva interface to place, size and style those elements you've added to your canvas.

4. When finished, share or download your design

  • In the top right menu, you can click Share to post to Facebook or Twitter or email your design
  • Or, click download to save your design as an image or a PDF
